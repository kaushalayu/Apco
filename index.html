What We Are

APCO Infratech Pvt. Ltd. is a dynamic and professionally managed organisation with a strong commitment to quality and safety, evidence by its certifications in ISO ISO 9001:2015 and OHSAS 45001:2018. Our company prides itself on its dedicated and highly skilled workforce, which exceeds 10,000 employees, including over 3,500 professionals with expertise spanning various critical domains.

Over the years, APCO has marked its presence in a wide range of sectors, achieving remarkable milestones in the construction and development of highways, metro, energy projects, tunnels, irrigation systems, urban infrastructure, and industrial development. In recent years, we have also expanded our portfolio to include complex water pipeline projects, innovative water tunnels, and marine infrastructure ventures.

Recognised as one of India's fastest-growing mid-sized infrastructure conglomerates, APCO has demonstrated robust growth sustained over three decades. Our enduring success is not only a testament to our strategic vision and operational excellence but also reflects our unwavering dedication to delivering high-quality infrastructure solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients and contribute to national development.
